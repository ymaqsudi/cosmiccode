Why Code?

Unlock your child's potential with the power of coding! In the digital age we live in, coding is no longer just a skill - it's a vital tool that allows your child to create, innovate, and transform the digital world around them. With coding knowledge, your child can start building their own apps, games, and websites, bringing their ideas to life. Whether your child is interested in pursuing a career in technology or simply wants to explore the fascinating world of programming, our tutoring services can provide the guidance and resources they need to succeed.

At Cosmic Coding, we firmly believe that every child has the potential to become a coding genius. That's why we are passionately dedicated to helping kids comprehend the fundamental concepts of software. Our tutors will guide your child step-by-step to develop a strong foundation in coding, equipping them with the tools and knowledge to unleash their creativity and bring their ideas to life. By introducing your child to basic coding concepts, we are paving the way for a lifetime of achievement. Whether your child's dream is to create the next big app, website, or video game, our tutoring services will help them achieve their goals. With our guidance, your child will become a brilliant programmer in no time!

About Me

Hi, I'm Yaseen! I am an undergraduate student at Stony Brook University, currently pursuing a double major in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science. I have a strong passion for teaching kids the power of code and have years of experience programming in a variety of languages, including C, Java, Python, R, and Javascript. I've built games, websites, mobile apps and worked for software company Cargo Matrix.

My background in robotics is strong, as I've programmed four FTC robots, including one that competed at the highest level in Houston, Texas. Also, I've completed bioinfomatic research on kidney disease, diabetes, and alternative herbicides for science journals. I am a strong proponent of bridging the fields of natural science and engineering with the software world. I believe the applications of coding go far beyond a text editor.

I've been coding since I was 8 and love sharing my passion for coding with kids. As a tutor, I help kids gain valuable coding skills and unlock their full potential.

Class Info

Welcome to our personalized Python tutoring class! Our 1-on-1 sessions are specifically designed to teach children the fundamental concepts of the Python programming language. Each session will last for approximately one hour and cost $35. To ensure our students benefit from our tutoring program, we offer the first session for free. Our class schedules are flexible, and we will work with you to find a convenient time that suits your schedule. As availability is limited and on a first-come-first-served basis, we encourage you to sign up today to secure your spot!

During each session, kids will learn the basics of Python and work on building exciting projects like 2D games, simple websites, and more. Our classes will be held over Google Meet to ensure a safe and interactive learning environment. To get the most out of our classes, students will need access to a computer and will need to install certain software.

We believe in a hands-on approach to learning, so while there will be minimal homework assignments and no exams, we will have project assignments to help students digest the material they've been learning. Our goal is to help kids discover the fun and creativity of coding while builiding a strong foundation in Python.
